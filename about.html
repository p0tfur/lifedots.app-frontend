About LifeDots.app

LifeDots.app is a unique visualization tool designed to help you understand and make the most of your time. By representing your life as a collection of dots, we provide a powerful visual metaphor for both the time you've lived and the time ahead.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people develop a better understanding of time and mortality, not to create anxiety but to inspire mindful living and purposeful decision-making.

How It Works

LifeDots.app uses your birth year and country-specific life expectancy data to create a personalized visualization of your life timeline. Each dot represents a unit of time, giving you a clear picture of your life's progression.

Features

  • Visual timeline representation
  • Country-specific life expectancy data
  • Real-time age and progress calculations
  • Customizable themes and appearance
  • Motivational insights

Privacy First

We respect your privacy. All calculations and data storage happen locally in your browser - we don't collect or store any personal information.
